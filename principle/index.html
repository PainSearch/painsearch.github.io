Principle of operation

1. Registration

For successful registration you need to enter a name, a surname, a mail and a password. After successful registration you will be directed to the main application page.(the password must be at least 6 characters long)

2. Login

To enter the application, you must enter the data entered earlier.

3. Profile

In the application you can change the name and surname entered at registration, as well as profile avatar.

4. Test

To pass the test, you need to select your gender, then the exciting part of the body and the interview, consisting of several questions. In the end, you will get the result: the cause of the pain and the doctors who specialize in this field.

5. History

All the results for convenience are recorded in history. The information in it is located most conveniently for the user.

6. Medicines

In the appendix there is a section in which popular medicines are collected. When clicking on the corresponding medicine, a page appears with a cheaper analogue, as well as the active substance of each medicine is indicated. Provided that the drug is prescription, there is a plate with the inscription "recipe drug".
